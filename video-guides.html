Nigerian Style Pizza

⏱️ 12:22 Mins⭐⭐ Medium

Key Steps to Remember:

  1. Let the yeast foam for 10 minutes before mixing with flour.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let it rise for 45 minutes until doubled in size.
  4. Bake at 400°F (200°C) until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Creamy Homemade Yogurt

⏱️ 10:15 Mins⭐⭐ Medium

Key Steps to Remember:

  1. Heat milk until it just simmers, then cool to lukewarm (110°F/43°C).
  2. Full cream milk and added powdered milk = thickest result. Low fat milk gives thinner yogurt.
  3. Cover tightly and keep in a warm place for 8-12 hours=.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours to thicken before serving.

Simple Vanilla Cream Cake

⏱️ 09:52 Mins⭐ Easy

Key Steps to Remember:

  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy for a tender cake.
  2. Warm cream will not whip. Use cream with at least 35% fat.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Whip heavy cream with powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.Dont use regular milk or evaporated milk.
  5. Keep refrigerated. Best eaten within 2 days. Always transport chilled.
