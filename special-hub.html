Banana Pancake

⏱️ 20 Mins 🍽️ 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 not too ripe bananas
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 onion(sliced)
  • 1 egg - egg whites would serve as natural raising agent
  • 1/2 sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk - powdered or liquid
  • 1 tsp butter - melted
  • Fresh pepper, nutmeg powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • Vannilla flavor

Instructions

  1. Mash the bananas in a large bowl until a smooth slightly watery paste is formed.
  2. In a seperate bowl whisk egg white and sugar until foamy.
  3. Add warm water to powdered milk and mix well
  4. In another bowl beat egg yolk properly, add fresh pepper, vanilla flavor, onions, salt, melted butter and powdered nutmeg. Mix well.
  5. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Do not overmix.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it. Add sliced onions and let it fry slightlynto enhance oil flavor.
  7. Fry the batter under very low heat and allow to fold form properly before flipping
  8. Serve warm with honey or maple syrup.
  9. TIP: Bananas are rich in sugar have 75% water and more mosit. Ensure to add less sweetners and liquid.

Plantain Pancake

⏱️ 25 Mins🍽️ 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe plantain
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg - egg whites would serve as your natural rising agent
  • 1/2 cup milk - liquid or powdered
  • 1 tsp butter - melted
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla flavour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tbsp powdered nutmeg
  • Fresh pepper & onions

Instructions

  1. Blend or mash plantains until a smooth paste is formed.
  2. In a seperate bowl beat egg whites and sugar until foamy.
  3. Add warm water to powdered milk and mix well
  4. In another bowl beat egg yolk properly, add fresh pepper, vanilla flavor, onions, salt, melted butter and powdered nutmeg. Mix well.
  5. Add warm milk to plantain paste and smoothen out properly.
  6. Slowly add all wet ingredients into the plaintain paste while mixing. A slightlyw watery paste should formed
  7. Add flour little by little into the watery paste and mix until a semi-thick texture is formed.
  8. Fry on a hot greased pan under low heat until golden brown on both sides. You can spice up frying oil with slices of onions and pepper to give the pancake a more richer taste
  9. Serve with your favorite topping!
  10. TIP: Ensure your batter has a semi-thick texture and fry under low heat so the pancake holds form.

Potato Pancake

️ ⏱️ 30 Mins🍽️ 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes (cut into small sized cubes)
  • 1/2 onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 egg - egg whites would serve as your natural raising agent
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp butter - melted
  • Salt, fresh pepper, and garlic powder
  • Banana & vanilla flavor
  • Nutmeg powder

Instructions

  1. Cook diced potatoes in water until half done, drain to remove excess water.
    2. \
  2. Mash the potatoes until a smooth/thickened paste is formed, leave to cool completely.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk egg whites and sugar until foamy and set aside.
  4. Add warm water to powdered milk and mix well
  5. Beat egg yolk properly, add fresh pepper, flavoring, salt, melted butter, garlic powder and powdered nutmeg. Mix well.
  6. Add liquid milk to mashed potaoes and mix well. Add the wet ingredients slowly and mix until a semi-watery paste is formed.
  7. Add flour in small batches to the paste, each time stirring on meduim until the mixture holds a slightly thick form. Batter must look thick.
  8. First fry the onions lightly and fold into batter. This prevents it from being overcooked since frying the batter takes longer time.
  9. Fry in small, thin patties with hot oil under low heat until crispy and golden on both sides.
  10. Drain on paper towels and serve hot!
  11. TIP: Potatoes are very moist so enusre to drain out excess water as much as possible. Too moist and it takes longer to fry.
