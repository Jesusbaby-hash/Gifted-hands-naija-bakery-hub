Party Small Chops

⏱️ 1.5 Hours 🍽️ Serves 8-10 ⭐⭐ Medium

Get ready to party with this iconic Nigerian finger food platter! Packed with a fiery, delicious variety of puff-puff, samosas, spring rolls, and peppered meats, it is the ultimate bite-sized celebration of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Filling: Diced carrots, cabbage, green peas, boiled eggs, curry, thyme, scotch bonnet.

Instructions

  1. Make a shortcrust pastry with flour, butter, sugar, egg, and baking powder. Chill for 20 mins.
  2. Sauté vegetables and diced eggs with spices until tender. Let it cool.
  3. Roll out dough, cut into circles, place in tart tins, and fill with vegetable mixture.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 mins until golden and crisp.

Layered Nigerian Parfait

⏱️ 15 Mins ⏱️ Chill Time: 1 hour 🍽️ Serves 4 large cups ⭐ Easy

Cool down with a vibrant, refreshing twist on a classic dessert. Layers of creamy yoghurt, crunchy granola, and fresh tropical fruits come together to create a sweet, energizing treat perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups(720ml) plain or Greek yogurt
  • 3 tbsp powdered milk(optional)
  • 1/2 cup granola or crushed biscuits
  • 1/4 cup mixed fruits (berries, banana, grapes, groundnuts/cashews)
  • 1 tbsp honey/condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk yoghurt until smooth. Stir in powdered milk, honey/condensed milk, and vanilla. Chill 30mins to thicken.
  2. Wash and dice all fruits into small-bite size cues. Pat dry with paper towel to avoid wetness.
  3. In clear cups or jars, build layers: Spoon 2-3 tbsp yoghurtat bottom. A layer of mixed fruit. A layer of granola and nuts. Repeat 2-3 times.
  4. Drizzle with honey and top with chia seeds. Chill for at least and 1 hour.

Loaded Nigerian Shawarma

⏱️ 45 Mins 🍽️ Serves 4 ⭐⭐ Medium

Indulge in Nigeria’s favorite street-side wrap, bursting with bold, smoky flavor. Grilled, spiced chicken is tightly wrapped in soft flatbread with fresh veggies and our signature creamy, sweet-and-spicy cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken tighs or breast(600g) - thinly sliced
  • Onion 1/2 small, grated
  • Garlic and Ginger 3 cloves minced & 1tsp, grated
  • Curry powder/shawarma spice 1tsp
  • Dried thyme & Black pepper 1/2tsp
  • Chicken seasoning cube 1 - crushed
  • Salt 1tsp
  • Vegetable oil & Lemon juice 2tbsp, 1 tbsp
  • 1/2 cup cheese slices (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup spicy mayo (mix mayo + ketchup + pepper)
  • Sliced onions & tomatoes
  • For Shawarma sauce:(sugar 1/2tsp, Chilli powder/ Hot sauce - to taste, Evaporated milk 1tbsp, mayonnaise 1/2cup)
  • For Assembly: Shawarma bread/large flour tortillas(6), Cabbage 2cups - finely shredded, Carrot 1 medium,julienned, Tomato 1 deseeded and sliced, Onion 1/2 medium, thinly sliced, Cucumber 1/2 cut into strips(optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix chicken with onions, garlic, ginger, all spices, seasoning cube, salt, oil and lemon juice. Cover and marinate 1 hour minimum, overnight is best.
  2. Sear chicken miz in a hot pan 6-8 mins until browned with charred edges. Chop into bite pieces.
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, ketchup,evaporated milk, sugar and chilli pe[er. It should be creamy, sweet-tangy.
  4. Toss cabbage,carrot,tomato, onions and cucumber together.
  5. Heat shawarma bread 20 seconds per side on a dry pan so it rolls without cracking.
  6. Assemble: Spread sauce on bread, add veggies, add chicken, drizzle more sauce. The fold the bottom up, roll tightly, wrap in foil.
  7. Toast seam-side down on a hot dry pan or sandwich maker for 2-3 mins per side to seal and crisp.
  8. Best served warm with your favorite drink!

Nigerian Style Pizza

⏱️ 1.5 Hours 🍽️ Serves 4-6 ⭐⭐ Medium

Ingredients

  • Dough: 3 cups flour, 1 tbsp yeast, 1 cup warm water, 2 tbsp oil, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar
  • Toppings: Tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, diced peppers, onions, sausage/chicken, sweet corn

Instructions

  1. Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it foam for 10 mins.
  2. Add flour, oil, and salt. Knead into a smooth dough. Let it rise for 45 mins.
  3. Roll out dough, place on a baking tray, and spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese, meats, and vegetables. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 mins until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Creamy Homemade Yogurt

⏱️ 20 Mins (+ 8-12 hrs chilling) 🍽️ Makes 1.5 Liters 6-8 servings ⭐⭐⭐ Advanced

Ingredients

  • Whole milk - 1.5 liters full-cream milk for creamiest result
  • Powdered milk 1/2 cup(60g) - optional, makes it richer and thicker
  • Sugar 1/2 - 1/2 cup(50-100g)
  • Plain yogurt 3 tbsp(45g) - with live cultures OR 1 sachet yogurt starter
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat milk in a pot until it just begins to simmer (do not boil). Whisk in powdered milk until no lumps. Bring to (about 185°F/85°C) just before boiling.
  2. Let milk cool to 43-46°C/110-115°F. This is important. Too hot milk kills starter. too cold and it wont ferment. To check without thermometer, dip a clean finger. It should feel like a warm bath water.
  3. Add starter: Take 1/2 cup of the warm milk, mix with the 3 tbsp of plain yoghurt until smooth. Pour that back into the pot. Add sugar and vanilla if using, then stir gently..
  4. Incubate: Pour into a clean bowl or flask with a tight lid. Wrap the container with a thick towel. Put it in a warm, draft-free place for 8-12 hours. Dont move or open during this time.
  5. After 8 hours, it should look set like soft jelly and smell tangy. If its still milky give it 2-4 more hours.
  6. Chill: Once set, stir gently to smoothen it out. For Greek style, strain through a cheesecloth for 2 hours. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before eating.
